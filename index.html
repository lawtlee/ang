HELLO!

Welcome to Ang's Kitchen. This little space on the web is where I share my favorite recipes—from the comforting dishes my grandma taught me to the creations I've whipped up in my own kitchen. It's a place filled with the flavors that mean the most to me, and I hope they'll find a special place in your heart (and your kitchen) too <3

ABOUT

Ang's Kitchen is a place born from a deep love for food and cherished memories from my grandma's cooking. This website is my personal collection of recipes that hold a special place in my heart. It's where I share the warmth of my kitchen and the stories behind each dish, from comforting family classics to new favorites I've discovered along the way.

Beyond my own recipes, you'll also find a carefully curated Guides section. Here, I've documented my favorite food spots from recent travels to Singapore and Japan—places so memorable that I had to write them down to share with you and remember for future adventures. This site isn't just for me; it's for my family, my friends, and for you—whether you're looking for inspiration in the kitchen or your next culinary adventure abroad. So, let's embark on this delicious journey together, creating and discovering joy one bite at a time.
