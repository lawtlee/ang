FOOL PROOF SUSHI BAKE

A foolproof crowd-pleaser that layers fluffy rice with creamy, spicy seafood, all topped off with avocado and baked until golden—a no-roll, all-thrill dish.

Ingredients

1 pack imitation crab

2 cups sushi rice

2 tbsp rice vinegar

furikake

1/3 cup kewpie mayo

1/4 cup sriracha

1 avocado

1 tbsp sugar

1 tbsp

1 egg

Instructions

1.

Pat steak dry with paper towels. Season both sides generously with salt, pepper, and Italian seasoning.

2.

Pour oil into a cast iron pan. Once heated, lay the steak into the pan away from you.

3.

Add in silken tofu block, break into pieces carefully with a spatula. Mix gently.

4.

Season with chicken bouillon, sugar, salt + pepper, and sesame oil. Turn heat to low.

5.

In a small bowl, combine corn starch with water 1:2 ratio. Mix well and gently pour this mixture into the pot.

6.

Add garnishes of sesame seeds and scallions.
